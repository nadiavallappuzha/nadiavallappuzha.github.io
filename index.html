About NADIA

NADIA students' union is a vibrant students' association of Darunnajath Islamic Complex Vallapuzha (affiliated to Darul Huda Islamic University, Chemmad) aiming the complete qualitative excellence of students. NADIA came to existence in 2006. Nadia portrays the path of opportunities which can be utilised for their academic progression. NADIA strengthens the calibres of students by providing them all facilities of developments according with the advancements of modern period. NADIA becomes a great motive for profound scholars to mainstream of present advanced world.

OUR MISSION & VISION

Our Mission

NADIA students'union aims to inspire a generation having various talents and calibres which make them cop up with the challenges of modern world and meet up with the exception of the society. It also emphasizes on the organisational expertise from their academic period and provides endeavours and innovations regarding with the contemporary significance.

Our Vision

NADIA's goal is to encourage students to participate in competitions and follow their passions. NADIA takes the initiative to be the precise driver of students' success. Nadia's dynamic is to facilitate all chances for students to acquire Islamic knowledge combined with current academics, with the goal of playing a significant part in raising the students to be polite and competent Islamic scholars.

Administrative Body

The current administrative body of NADIA consists of six core committee members and executive members followed by officially selected members. The whole administrative body would meet once in a month to discuss the future schemes, evaluate current performance and enhance the programs.

SUB COMMITTE

SRDB

SRDB is renowned as the corner stone of NADIA for bearing the keyhole position and executing the most relevant programs of among other wings of NADIA. It focuses on improving the resources of studentsin curricular and co-curricular fields. It has the duty of improving students' potentials by conducting programs related to all aspects of arts. Four language wings, GK& Science club and art wing are working under SRDB.

ENGLISH HUB

The official English promoting wing of NADIA, under SRDB. It conducts programs related with English language. It publishes language newspaper fortnightly and language magazine on relevant issues. Also make students experts in the English language by conducting programs to improve their listening, reading, speaking and writing in English.

MALAYALIKKUTTAM

This is the official Malayalam wing of NADIA to organize programs consentrated in Malayalam language. It conducts to improve the linguistic abilities of students in Malayalam like assemblies and grammatic expertise and it plays key role in enhancing the talent.

RABITHATHUL ARABIYYA

This is the official Arabic wing of the union. It focuses on conducting Arabic language based programs by organising varities of programs. The prestigious Munazra and other contests are conducted under this wing. This wing is implementing sufficient plans to improve the credentials pertaining to the language under SRDB.

URDU AND HINDI WING

The official Urdu and Hindi wing of NADIA emphasize on Urdu and Hindi based programs in its various genres. Publishing newspaper fortnightly and Urdu and Hindi language magazines are the responsibility of this wing. Under SRDB it conducts programs co-operated with other language wings.

ART CLUB

It targets to promote the artistic skills of students like painting and drawings. It opens the world of creativity strengthening thinking abilities and comprehensive skills. Everyone who likes drawing should be encouraged and guided to expose their underlying skills and themes through the art of drawing. The mentoring is accomplished by providing numerous opportunities and workshops for students in cartoon, calligraphy, designing and other art related fields.

GK AND SCIENCE CLUB

This wing strands for empowering the scientific tendencies and general awareness of students co- ordinated by SRDB through various programs like science expo, master challenge,etc. This wing becomes more aware of the understandings of students upon the golden Islamic age of scientific contribution and pawing out the complete path of progress for those who are interested in the field mathematics is also concerned under this wing.

EDITORIAL BOARD

The wing stands to connect students with online journals and magazines to publish their works via social media. It emphasizes on informing students the public programs like paper presentations and other competitive calls. The duty of publishing the prime monthly magazine of the college named 'KIRANAM' is vested in the wing. Besides that it assists media wing to accelerate publicity of programs of NADIA by publishing it in newspapers and other social medias.

PUBLISHING BUREAU

To maintain the social and academic representation of NADIA through publications is vested in publishing bureau. It takes initiative to publish booklets and pamphlets regarding with special events and celebrations. The book of students are also published under this wing. Ith is an inevitable part of NADIA, maintaining economic stability through supplimentries and booklets.

SOCIAL AFFAIRS

It works to promote the preservation of environment in campus surroundings collaborated with organizing wing. It arranges the venue settings, mic operations and other requirements for programs and conducts 'clean campus green campus' mission and maintain the cleanliness of campus.

LIBRARY BOARD

The complete responsibility of Kamaludheen Usthad digital library is vested in library board. It maintains the library books and hall along with taking initiatives to enlarge the reading world of students by providing books, category-wise it ensures the functional use of newspapers and periodicals in the reading room. It also emphasizes on conducting the splendid program 'The Reader Of The Year' annually and other competitions like book reviews, book shows etc. It is also aware of bringing necessary books to library.

MEDIA WING

Media wing of NADIA pays attention to enrich the technical and designing skills of students. It manages the official social media accounts of NADIA like Facebook, YouTube and Instagram and maintaining the responsibility of creating publicity through social media platforms for programs conducted by NADIA. They also create posters of NADIA programs conducted by core/sub committees making reports and snapshots of programs.

DEPT. OF QUR'AN & DA'WA CELL

Collaborated with the Department of Qur'an in DNIC, DA'WA cell conducts programs related with spiritual empowerment and Qur'anic teachings and the memorial days of personalities, it has to conduct memorial speeches other evoking programs. It organizes Dhikr Halqa monthly to enhance the devotional strength of students cooperated with class organizations. It takes initiative to convey Islamic messages to northern areas of Palakkad, where the religious interferences are not systematic.

PRASANGA KALAVEDI

Prasanga kalavedi is the wing to control PKV periods and masjid speeches to make orators and facilitate students to perform their abilities. It works to enhance the oral skills of students in terms of speaking and other oral abilities. It conducts programs regularly in a day of a week and also some colourful competition occasionally.

SPORTS WING

The wing stands to promote the physical strength and well-being of students and ensure the opportunities for playing under this wing, many sports items are conducted and 'SPORTEENO' trophy is presented to the champion of sports. It focuses on making students keen, capable and vibrant for academic activness.

MEDICAL BOARD

Medical board plays a key role in keeping students healthy. It provides the first aid and adequate treatment for the patients. Besides offering services for the patients, it conducts health awareness programs and free medical checkup in collaboration with outside organizations occasionally. It also conducts programs making students mentally healthy like self- meditation and stress relax classes.
